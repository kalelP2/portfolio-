EmotiMatch Project

EmotiMatch Prototype | Design Overview

This is the second app challenge we took on, focused on addressing emotional well-being. EmotiMatch aims to connect people struggling with their emotions, allowing them to find new friends with similar experiences or connect with mentors and therapists. The app includes a minigame where players control a smiley face to "eat" angry faces, symbolizing managing negative emotions.

Team Pathways Project

Team Pathways Prototype | Design Overview

Created as part of an Infosys app challenge, this project focuses on helping young people explore future career paths. The goal is to make finding and applying for jobs easier and more aligned with their interests and skills.
