EmotiMatch Prototype
EmotiMatch Design

This is the second app challenge we did. This social issue we did is about Emotions. We did emotions because people dont know how to control their emotions. They end up feeling isolated and alone. So our app is to connect people that have problems with their emotions. In our app you can talk to new people who have the same issues and make new friends or you can talk to mentor's/therapist. We have a minigame where you play as a smiley face and eat the angry faces and after you eat them all you win.
